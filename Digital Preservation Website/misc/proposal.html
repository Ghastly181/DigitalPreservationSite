Topic-description Digital Preservation is the act of preserving current and past artifacts digitally to ensure that later on in the future they will be accessible for study. This is important to ensure that various works of art and cultural heritage stay intact for future generations to view and experience in some form. Without Digital Preservation it would be nearly impossible for future generations to see how the world looks digitally and physically at this time.
